 I am a 2nd year PhD student in Electronic Engineering at the University of York. This is my personal website which I will try to keep up to date with my research activities. In my research I am looking at the applications of artificial intelligence in control engineering and radio resource management in cellular networks.

Please see my CV, list of publications and my departmental project webpage for more details about my work. I have also implemented an interactive inverted pendulum control demo. Hopefully, there will be a few more of them in the future.
