Research Experience

PhD research
University of York
Oct 2012 - present		 Looking at applications of artificial intelligence and control engineering to radio resource management in future cognitive cellular systems. This work is funded by the European FP7 ABSOLUTE project.
MEng project
University of York
Jan 2012 - Jun 2012		 My MEng project focused on investigating reinforcement learning techniques and their application in control of the rotary inverted pendulum. The primary aim of this project was to develop a real-time reinforcement learning algorithm which would learn a robust pendulum swing-up and balance controller only by learning from system’s interaction with the environment.
Research placement
University of York
Jun 2011 - Sep 2011		 I have received an EPSRC scholarship to undertake a 10-week research placement in control engineering at University of York. During this placement I was working individually on a research project. I have developed a full state feedback controller for stabilizing the rotary inverted pendulum, using a PIC microcontroller as the processing unit. I have also developed an energy based controller for the swing up of the inverted pendulum.
